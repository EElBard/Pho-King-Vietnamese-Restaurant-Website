Our Story

Pho, Done Right

Since 2017, Phở King Vietnamese Kitchen has been bringing the warmth and soul of Vietnamese cooking to Las Vegas. We believe great food starts with great ingredients — nothing less.

Every bowl of phở we serve is built on a broth simmered low and slow with aromatic spices, topped with tender cuts and the freshest herbs. From phở to rice dishes and beyond, every dish is a love letter to Vietnamese culinary tradition.
